Step 1: Draw out anything, on anything, using anything. Easy.

Step 2: Take a photo or scan your drawing to get it onto your computer and open it with Paint.net (Free!). Use the magic wand tool to select the bits you want to remove and delete as necessary. Begin to fill the lines you have remaining using the fill tool.

Step 3: Fill the areas that you want colored in using the fill tool. If you get any colors leaking into areas that you do not want, Zoom in and close the gap in the section making use of the zoom features. Boom!
